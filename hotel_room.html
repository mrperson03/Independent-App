Best Places to Stay in Vancouver!




Hilton Vancouver Downtown

They are next to Yaletown, a neighborhood in Downtown Vancouver, with its buzzing restaurants, boutiques, and bars, and close to all the major concert and sports venues. They offer mainly one-bedroom suites, where you'll have plenty of space to relax. Take in the views of iconic Robson Street from their year-round rooftop heated pool and enjoy steam rooms, saunas and fitness center.



Fairmont Waterfront

This hotel is a 4-minute walk from Waterfront Station, and this stylish hotel with city, mountain and harbour views is 5 minutes' stroll from Canada Place convention centre. With floor-to-ceiling windows providing scenic views, the premium rooms feature HDTVs, free Wi-Fi and coffee machines. Upgraded quarters feature high ceilings and terraces, luxe suites add separate living rooms, as well as wet bars and/or whirlpool baths. Some rooms feature access to a club lounge with drinks and food. There's 24-hour room service. A great restaurant has harbour views and a bar with live music. There are meeting rooms, a ballroom and a heated outdoor pool, plus a health club.



Rosewood Hotel Georgia

Embark on a journey with Rosewood Hotels & Resorts, the world's finest array of luxury hotels and resorts inspired by elegant wanderlust. They have free wi-fi and parking. They are also wheelchair accessible.
